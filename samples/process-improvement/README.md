# Process Improvement & Automation

A selection of workflow improvements built to eliminate manual steps, reduce reporting lag, and create self-serve tools for Finance and cross-functional teams.

---

### Finance Operations Calendar

A centralised, automated calendar to manage global reporting deadlines across time zones. Designed to ensure 100% compliance with close cycles and proactively surface housekeeping tasks — such as expiring subscriptions and vendor contract renewals.

![Finance Operations Calendar](Finance%20Dynamic%20Calendar%20GIF.gif)

---

### Dynamic Reporting with Pivot Charts & Slicers

Replaced static, tab-heavy reports with a single source of truth enhanced with Pivot Charts, slicers, and timelines — enabling self-serve navigation for non-finance stakeholders without requiring manual refreshes or analyst support.
